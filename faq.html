Frequently Asked Questions
How do I create an account?
Click on the Sign In button and select 'Create Account'. Fill in your details and choose your role to get started.
How do I buy a product?
Browse the products, click 'Buy Now', and follow the payment instructions. You must be logged in to complete a purchase.
What payment methods are supported?
We support Card, eSewa, and Khalti payments for your convenience.
How do I add products to my cart?
Click the 'Add to cart' button on any product. You can view your cart at any time from your dashboard.
How do I contact support?
You can reach out to our support team via the Contact Us section or email us at support@techspark.com.
Can I sell my own products?
Yes! Register as a seller and start listing your products after approval.
